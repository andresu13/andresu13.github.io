Our Services

Each session is one-on-one, tailored to meet your child’s needs. All sessions require an evaluation or screening prior to scheduling. Duration of recommended therapy time may vary depending on your child’s specific needs. Sessions may be held virtually, at home, or in school.

Language

Language therapy helps children understand what they hear or read and helps them to use words to tell others what they are thinking. A child's language therapy needs are determined by a language evaluation, and a specific plan of care is created especially for each child based upon their needs.

Articulation

Articulation therapy teaches children to say sounds correctly in words and connected speech. A child may need articulation therapy due to an articulation disorder, a phonological disorder, or due to childhood apraxia of speech. An articulation evaluation is completed to determine if a disorder is present, the type of disorder that is present, and what treatment approach is needed.

Social Communication

Pragmatic language therapy (social communication) targets social skills such as: following directions, using eye contact when speaking and when spoken to, engaging in joint attention with others, forming appropriate responses and conversation, remaining engaged in tasks, engaging in functional play, and much more.
